about my page

This page is designed and coded by me. Architecture folks are notorious for having confusing or difficult websites, so if something seems wrong please let me know!

I think it's really important that everyone knows a little bit about how websites work. As we increasingly rely on a handful of apps and websites, it's good to know that you can actually still design your own homepage. You can post what you want, share it with who you want, and make it feel like your own.

You don't need to know a lot to make your own space online. In fact, you could probably learn enough to design this site in less than a week. W3 Schools is a great place to start.
