gospel swamp

master's thesis • princeton university, 2023 • advised by prof. marshall brown

Gospel Swamp proposes a future for Garden Grove, California. It doubles down on the backyard as the heart of the city, amplifying the many activities occurring in these dynamic, cultural spaces. It imagines the backyard not as personal property but as a shared civic infrastructure.
